## ğŸ® How to Play

### Objective
Rearrange the colored tiles on the ladder to match the target pattern shown at the top. Plan your moves carefully - each action shifts multiple tiles!

### Controls

**Desktop & Mobile:**
- **Click/Tap on Rung** - Flip that section of the ladder
- **Undo Button** - Reverse last move
- **Reset Button** - Start level over
- **Hint Button** - Get a suggested move

### Game Rules
- The ladder has colored tiles arranged on horizontal rungs
- Clicking a rung flips the tiles on that rung and affects adjacent rungs
- Match the target pattern at the top to complete the level
- Try to solve in minimum moves for best score
- More complex patterns unlock as you progress

### How Moves Work
When you click a rung:
1. Tiles on that rung swap positions
2. Adjacent rungs are also affected
3. The pattern cascades through the ladder
4. Plan ahead to reach the goal efficiently
